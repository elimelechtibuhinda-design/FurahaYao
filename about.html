About FurahaYao Safaris

We are passionate about sharing the wonders of Tanzania with the world. At FurahaYao Safaris, we believe that every journey should be as unique as the traveler. Our team is dedicated to making your dream adventure a reality, whether you are seeking the thrill of a wildlife safari, the serenity of a beach escape, or the cultural richness of Tanzania’s vibrant communities. From the moment you contact us, we take care of every detail—planning, logistics, and support—so you can focus on enjoying your adventure and making lifelong memories. We pride ourselves on our local expertise, warm hospitality, and commitment to delivering truly unforgettable experiences for every guest.

Our Services

  • Custom safari planning and organization – Personalized itineraries for every traveler.
  • Experienced local guides – Knowledgeable, friendly, and passionate about Tanzania.
  • Transport & accommodation – We arrange everything for a smooth journey.
  • Wildlife & nature tours – Explore the Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Zanzibar, and more.
  • Cultural & historical excursions – Discover Tanzania’s rich heritage and traditions.
  • Beach holidays & island getaways – Relax on Zanzibar’s white sands or enjoy water sports.
  • Airport transfers & city tours – Seamless arrivals, departures, and guided city visits.
  • Family, group, and solo packages – Options for every type of traveler.
  • 24/7 support – We’re always here for you during your trip.
